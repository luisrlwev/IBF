Mission & Objectives

Mission

Since reinventing financial services education in 1988, our mission at IBF has been to enhance the professionalism of financial advisors through designation, certification, and degree programs that foster integrity, a commitment to continuous improvement, and excellent client service.

We monitor and ensure fulfillment of our mission through an ongoing analysis of student learning outcomes and an in-depth review of student satisfaction.

Objectives

In pursuit of our mission, we at IBF strive to deliver knowledge and tools that are

  • above and beyond what is required for licensure
  • convenient and accessible for working professionals via our state-of-the-art online education model
  • current and actionable across a full spectrum of the financial services industry
  • founded in advanced theory and applicable to real-world practice
  • supportive of ethical behavior, fiduciary responsibility, and ongoing professional development
  • recognized in the industry as degrees and designations of distinction, quality, and commitment to the highest professional standards
